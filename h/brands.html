Miso Brands

In Osaka

Major miso brands (common in Japanese supermarkets around Osaka)

  1. Marukome (マルコメ) — white (shiro), red (aka), awase (blends), reduced-salt, instant miso soup.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.marukome.co.jp/global/en/
  2. Hikari Miso (ひかり味噌) — organic lines, white/red/awase, low-salt, specialty koji miso.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://hikarimiso.com/
  3. Miyasaka / Shinsyu-ichi (ミヤサカ醸造 / 信州一) — Shinshu-style rice miso (white/red/awase), premium aged types.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://miko-brand.com/
  4. Hanamaruki (ハナマルキ) — white, red, awase, instant miso soup, no-additive lines.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.hanamaruki.co.jp/english/
  5. Kikkoman (キッコーマン) — miso soup mixes (instant/powder), packaged miso products.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.kikkoman.com/
  6. Otafuku Foods (おたふく) — miso sauces (miso-based marinades and prepared miso sauces).
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://otafukufoods.com/
  7. Yamato Soy Sauce & Miso Co., Ltd. (ヤマト醤油味噌) — artisan / regional miso (Kanae organic line).
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.yamato-soysauce-miso.co.jp/English/
  8. Ishino / Kyoto-style miso (石野味噌・京白味噌) — Kyoto-style sweet white miso, specialty regional miso.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.kyoto-ishino.jp/
  9. Yamajirushi (ヤマ印 / 信州味噌) — Shinshu miso (aka, awase), ramen miso bases.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.yamajirushi.co.jp/
  10. Mishima Foods (三島食品) — packaged seasonings and miso-based products.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.mishima.co.jp/
  11. Morita (盛田 / Morita Foods) — artisanal miso lines (tamari, soybean miso, regional varieties).
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.morita.jp/
  12. Ishii Miso (石井味噌) — small-batch miso brewery, Shinshu-style miso.
    Manufacturer / brand site: https://www.ishiimiso.com/
  13. Hatcho Miso (八丁味噌 / カクキュー・まるや八丁味噌) — pure soybean miso from Aichi Prefecture, also sold in Kansai supermarkets.
    Manufacturer / brand sites: https://www.kakukyu.jp/ and https://www.8miso.co.jp/
