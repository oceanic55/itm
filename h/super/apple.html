Apple Umeda

Apple Umeda is located on the 2nd floor of the South Wing of Grand Front Osaka. The nearest stations of Grand Front Osaka are Osaka Station (JR Line) and Osaka Umeda Station (Hankyu and Hanshin lines). The parking lot is available in Grand Front Osaka.

Apple Umeda is the second Apple store to open in Osaka and the 11th to open in Japan. It is located at 4-20 Ofukacho in the Kita ward of Osaka, next to the Osaka Station.

Apple Shinsaibashi

This Apple store location is in close proximity to the newly refurbished and classically retained Daimaru Dept. Store and is a hop, skip and a jump from the Dotonbori area.

The Apple Store (Shinsaibashi) opened its doors in 2004, becoming the first Apple Store in Japan. Since then, it has become a popular destination for Apple fans and technology enthusiasts. The store has undergone several renovations over the years, including a major redesign in 2017 that added a fourth floor and expanded the retail space.
